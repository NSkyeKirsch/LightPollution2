What is Light pollution?

According to the International Dark Sky Association: Light pollution is "the inappropriate or excessive use of artificial light... The fact that much outdoor lighting used at night is inefficient, overly bright, poorly targeted, improperly shielded, and, in many cases, completely unnecessary. This light, and the electricity used to create it, is being wasted by spilling it into the sky, rather than focusing it on to the actual objects and areas that people want illuminated."
